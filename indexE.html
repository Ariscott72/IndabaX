IndabaX Conference 2022

This conference is scheduled for December 14-15, 2022 at Félix Houphouet Boigny University in Abidjan, Côte d'Ivoire.

To participate

The world is experiencing its fourth industrial revolution driven by digital transformation and artificial intelligence.

This conference aims to popularize, promote and facilitate the development of artificial intelligence in Ivory Cost in order to make it a lever for the development process of our country.
